2022 Iowa PDE Conference


Saturday, October 22, 2022
Department of Mathematics

Click here for the schedule of this conference.

This is an IMA-PI conference, which will focus on the analysis and applications of partial differential equations, harmonic analysis. Interdisciplinary aspects of the subject will be emphasized, particularly the interactions between the theory and applications such as math biology, traffic flows, image processing, PDE-based modeling and numerical computations.

The mission of this meeting is to bring together experts, young researchers and graduate students to present and understand recent research advances, discover new research directions and foster future collaborations. Faculty, graduate students, and postdocs from PIs are eligible to use their IMA-PI funds for travel to the conference. A diverse group of students and faculty including Underrepresented Minority (URM) attendees are encouraged to attend the conference.
